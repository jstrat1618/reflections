When would you want to create a remote repo rather than keeping all your work local?
    When you want other people to contribute to it remotely.

Why might you want to always pull changes manually rather than having Git automatically stay up-to-date with your remote repository?
  Because you may not want to introduce those changes. For example, you may accidently introduce a bug.
  
 Describe the differences between forks, clones, and branches. When would you use one instead of another?
	Cloning is done on your local machine. Forking is done on GitHub.
